Proof Engine

Claims must become evidence before they scale.

BrandPresenceOS turns proof into a governed asset: verified claims, case studies, screenshots, outcomes, internal notes, and claims that should not go public yet.

Claim status

Every claim gets classified.

Verified

Approved numbers, screenshots, client permission, anonymized evidence, or public records ready for use.

Needs Proof

Promising claim that needs documentation before being used on high-visibility pages.

Internal Only

Useful for strategy or sales prep, but not public copy until cleared.

Remove

Claim is too vague, too risky, untrue, unverifiable, or off-brand.

Proof grounds

WGAI proof comes from three lanes.

WGAI Core

Agent orchestration, workflow governance, AI Ops, internal systems, source-of-truth operations, and BPOS build evidence.

Status: package first.

ACS Service Lane

Service-business proof for lead capture, local presence, CRM follow-up, operations, reporting, and cleaning-company infrastructure.

Status: collect and verify.

WADE Leader Lane

Brand authority proof for leadership messaging, audience capture, offers, events, assessment paths, and education/community conversion.

Status: separate from WGAI delivery claims.

Case study template

Proof should explain the system, not just the outcome.

Each case study should show the starting problem, system installed, tools connected, proof collected, result, and what was learned.

Required fields

  • Client / proof ground
  • Before state
  • System installed
  • Automation and CRM path
  • Evidence attached
  • Outcome status
  • Public claim approved or not
