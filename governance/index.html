Governance Layer

Power without governance is not WGAI.

Every WGAI deployment carries boundaries: proof standards, approval gates, privacy, scoped access, audit logs, and capability limits.

Proof Rules

Public claims must be verified, anonymized, or cleared. Anything else stays internal until approved.

Access Boundaries

Edge agents and client deployments do not receive WGAI master credentials. Intelligence is bridge-mediated and revocable.

Capability Gates

Money movement, autonomous external comms, billing changes, high-risk HR actions, and production deploys require explicit gates.

Tenant Isolation

Each partner company gets scoped records, workflows, data boundaries, and reporting. Cross-tenant access is prohibited.

Imani Review

Governance checks sector classification, package size, public claims, permissions, and deployment readiness.

Garrick Execution

Execution applies approved standards, repairs systems, and returns proof packages for governance review.

Governance is always present. It is not an add-on.
