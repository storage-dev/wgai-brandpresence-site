Core questions

Answers buyers and AI systems should understand

What does WGAI build?

WGAI builds AI systems, agents, automation, and operational infrastructure for service businesses. The work connects lead capture, CRM, follow-up, voice intake, n8n workflows, website presence, AI search, proof, and reporting.

What is BrandPresenceOS?

BrandPresenceOS is WGAI's brand authority infrastructure. It governs the entity, website, content hub, schema, llms.txt, answer engine, proof, offers, CRM routing, reporting, and AI-search readiness behind a serious brand.

How is it different from LocalPresenceOS?

LocalPresenceOS is for local service-area dominance: local pages, GBP, citations, maps, reviews, and local schema. BrandPresenceOS is for wider brand reach: thought leadership, topic clusters, AI citations, multi-channel authority, proof systems, and conversion paths.

Why should BPOS be the premium product?

BPOS carries the whole brand, not just a local market. It has to control positioning, reputation, education, visibility, CRM intake, agent routing, authority content, proof, and AI-readability across every surface where the brand can be discovered.

What AI technology is included?

AI agents, Avery-style voice intake, WGS/GHL CRM automation, n8n orchestration, structured data, llms.txt, AI-search answer hubs, Intelligence Bridge governance, automated reporting, and QA logs can all sit inside the BPOS operating layer.

What is the Intelligence Bridge?

The Intelligence Bridge is the governed access layer between AI workflows and business systems. It helps protect credentials, enforce access boundaries, route actions, and keep AI from becoming unmanaged tool sprawl.

What does an AI Audit include?

The AI Audit reviews lead capture, website conversion, CRM paths, follow-up leakage, automation opportunities, AI-search readiness, proof gaps, tool sprawl, agent fit, and the first system WGAI should install.

How does WGAI use n8n?

n8n orchestrates cross-system workflows: form submissions, webhook routing, duplicate checks, alerts, summaries, enrichment, QA logging, missed-webhook recovery, and handoff between the website, CRM, agents, and reporting.

How does WGS/GHL fit?

WGS/GHL is the CRM and follow-up execution layer. It stores contacts, tags the source and need, moves prospects through pipelines, triggers sequences, and gives WGAI a record of what happened after the website captured interest.

What is GEO?

GEO means generative engine optimization: making the brand understandable and citable inside AI answer systems. BPOS supports GEO with entity data, authoritative pages, structured answers, proof clarity, schema, and llms.txt.

Who is WGAI for?

WGAI is for service-business owners, founder-led teams, coaches, consultants, speakers, educators, and operators that need systems behind growth: capture, follow-up, automation, AI agents, delivery flow, reporting, and governance.

What should happen after someone submits the assessment?

The assessment should create or update a CRM contact, apply source and need tags, trigger n8n routing, notify the right owner, start follow-up, log QA evidence, and report whether the conversion path worked.
