Automation Blueprint

The website must trigger the operating system.

BPOS is not only pages and AI-search files. The WGAI site needs intake, CRM routing, n8n orchestration, voice support, follow-up, QA, and reporting so every serious visitor becomes a managed next step.

Conversion engine

What should happen after a visitor raises their hand

The automation blueprint turns website interest into a trackable system record. It keeps the site aligned with the original WGAI requirement: educate, collect, route, follow up, and measure.

Capture the signal

The visitor submits an assessment, calls the voice agent, books a strategy session, or completes an audit form.

Create or update the contact

WGS/GHL stores identity, company, industry, current tools, AI usage, bottleneck, desired result, source, and routing lane.

Classify the need

The system decides whether the next move is CRM repair, follow-up automation, voice intake, LocalPresenceOS, BrandPresenceOS, AI audit, or operating map.

Trigger orchestration

n8n checks duplicates, applies tags, sends notifications, stores summaries, and starts the correct follow-up workflow.

Close the loop

Reporting shows submitted leads, booked assessments, routed needs, follow-up status, failures, and conversion movement.

System layers

Automation infrastructure behind the site

Website Intake

Assessment fields, source fields, routing lane, landing-page context, event context, and consent-safe contact information.

WGS/GHL CRM

Contact creation, custom fields, pipeline stage, source tag, service lane tag, owner assignment, and follow-up sequence.

n8n Orchestration

Webhook handling, duplicate checks, enrichment, notifications, failure handling, audit logs, and cross-system handoff.

Avery Voice Layer

Calls, intake questions, transcripts, post-call summaries, and CRM updates for people who would rather talk than fill out a form.

Notion Registry

Workflow design, automation registry, QA proof, SOPs, ownership, and maintenance notes so the system can be repaired and repeated.

Scorecard

Lead volume, assessment requests, booked calls, follow-up completion, failure alerts, AI-search readiness, and proof readiness.

Production requirements

The current static form is only the placeholder.

The final deployed site should replace mailto handling with a governed endpoint or CRM form embed, then test the full path from submit to contact record to follow-up.

RouteConnect website assessment and audit forms to WGS/GHL and n8n.
TagApply source, event, need, business type, and service lane tags consistently.
AlertNotify the owner or assigned operator when a high-intent lead comes in.
RecoverAdd failure alerts, missed webhook checks, and manual fallback instructions.
ProveKeep QA logs and reporting so WGAI can show the system is working.

  • Primary need, current bottleneck, and desired result.
